Who am i?

  • Ricardo Nunes

    Front-End Developer

    Senior Front-End Developer with 7 years expertise.

A self-taught by nature, I gained a passion for design in a course of Computer Applications at my School, where I had the first contact with Photoshop and Flash.

It was like love at first sight, which made me work hard and passionately, giving me the highest degree in that course (20). Since then, my love for design and the web grew and I grew with it.

I spent 5 years as the main Web designer and Front-End Developer/marketeer/copy in a software house.

Right now I’m working as a Front-End Developer with two other people (creative director and a graphic designer), where we try to push the standards in each new website we publish for the company (parallax, responsive, mobile, CSS3, HTML5 animations and so on).

I’m always looking for the Next big thing in web-related and trying to push myself over my limits.
